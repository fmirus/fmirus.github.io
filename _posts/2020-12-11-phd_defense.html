Friday, December 11th, I successfully defended my Ph.D. thesis Towards a cognitive automotive environment model: a novel approach based on distributed representations and spiking neural networks. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the oral examination had to happen virtually via Zoom. I would like to thank the members of my committee, my supervisor Prof. Jorg Conradt, my second advisor Prof. Andreas Herkersdorf and the chairman Prof. Werner Hemmert. Furthermore, I would like to thank Dr. Hans-Jörg Vögel, my supervisor at BMW and Dr. Terry Stewart, for mentoring and supporting my doctoral research.
