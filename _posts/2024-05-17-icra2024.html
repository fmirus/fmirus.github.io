From May 13th to 17th, I had the pleasure to attend the 41st IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA), one of the most prestigious conferences in robotics, held in Yokohama, Japan. I presented our paper Towards Fault-Tolerant Deployment of Mobile Robot Navigation in the Edge: An Experimental Study both, as a talk as well as at the interactive poster session. Our work on edge-robotics was well received and appreciated by the robotics community sparking interesting and fruitful discussions. Finally, it was great meeting old friends and making new connections.
