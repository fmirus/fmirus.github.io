From September 17th to September 19th, I had the pleasure to attend the 28th International Conference on Artificial Neural Networks (ICANN) held in Munich. There were a lot of interesting research sessions and I had the opportunity to present our paper A mixture-of-experts model for vehicle prediction using an online learning approach  during one of the sessions. If you are interested in this research, get the paper online or feel free to contact me.
