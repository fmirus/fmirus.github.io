From July 19th to 24th, I had the pleasure to attend the 2020 World Congress on Computational Intelligence held virtually. There were a lot of interesting research sessions and I had the opportunity to present our papers The Importance of Balanced Data Sets: Analyzing a Vehicle Trajectory Prediction Model based on Neural Networks and Distributed Representations and Analyzing the Capacity of Distributed Vector Representations to Encode Spatial Information during the sessions. If you are interested in this research, feel free to contact me.
