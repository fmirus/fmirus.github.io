Our papers The Importance of Balanced Data Sets: Analyzing a Vehicle Trajectory Prediction Model based on Neural Networks and Distributed Representations  and Analyzing the Capacity of Distributed Vector Representations to Encode Spatial Information  were accepted at the IEEE International Joint Conference on Neural Networks (IJCNN), which will be held as part of the IEEE World Congress on Computational Intelligence (IEEE WCCI) in Glasgow.
