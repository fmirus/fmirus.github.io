I'm happy to share that our paper Multivariate time Series Anaysis for Driving Style Classification using neural Networks and Hyperdimensional Computing authored by Kenny Schlegel, myself, Peer Neubert and Peter Protzel has been accepted to the 32nd IEEE Intelligent Vehicles Symposium 2021. If you are interested in this research, you can find the source code on Github. I will share a link to the paper once it is published.
